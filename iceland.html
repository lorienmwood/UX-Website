    • ICELAND RING ROAD ITINERARY - 7-DAY ROAD TRIP + MAPS

    Embark on an unforgettable road trip around Iceland's Ring Road, a journey that promises breathtaking landscapes, cascading waterfalls, and otherworldly experiences. Here's a detailed 7-day itinerary to help you make the most of your adventure.

    Day 1: Reykjavik to Golden Circle

    Start your journey in Reykjavik and head to the iconic Golden Circle. Explore the historic Thingvellir National Park and the Silfra Fissure, witness erupting geysers at the Geysir Geothermal Area, and marvel at the powerful two-tiered Gullfoss Waterfall. Overnight in the Golden Circle area.

    Day 2: South Coast Wonders

    Experience a breathtaking drive along the stunning South Coast. Discover the magnificent Seljalandsfoss Waterfall, where you have the opportunity to walk behind the cascading water. Marvel at the impressive 60-meter high Skogafoss Waterfall and explore the extraordinary black sand beach and basalt columns at Reynisfjara. Enjoy an unforgettable overnight stay in the charming village of Vik.

    Day 3: Vatnajokull National Park

    Immerse yourself in the awe-inspiring natural wonders of Vatnajökull National Park. Embark on an exhilarating hike along the impressive Fjaðrárgljúfur Canyon, where you'll be surrounded by breathtaking views at every turn. Explore the enchanting trails and be captivated by the majestic Svartifoss waterfall in Skaftafell. Prepare to be amazed as you witness the surreal sight of floating icebergs at the iconic Jökulsárlón Glacier Lagoon. Take a leisurely stroll along Diamond Beach, where glistening ice chunks create a truly magical atmosphere. After a day filled with extraordinary experiences, unwind and rejuvenate in the charming Höfn area, ready to continue your Icelandic adventure.

    Day 4: East Fjords

    Embark on an unforgettable scenic drive along the breathtaking East Fjords, where you'll be captivated by the stunning landscapes and charming fishing villages. Immerse yourself in the fascinating world of Petra's Stone Collection in Stodvarfjordur, a unique experience that showcases the region's rich cultural heritage. Don't miss the opportunity to visit the vibrant and colorful village of Seydisfjordur, known for its artistic charm. And to complete your adventure, indulge in a peaceful night's rest surrounded by the tranquil beauty of the East Fjords.

    Day 5: North Iceland

    Immerse yourself in the awe-inspiring power of Dettifoss, Europe's most formidable waterfall. Embark on an exploration of the breathtaking horseshoe-shaped Asbyrgi Canyon and indulge in the unforgettable experience of a whale watching tour in Husavik, the renowned capital of whale watching. Unwind and rejuvenate in the captivating surroundings of the Akureyri area as you spend a memorable night in this enchanting region.

    Day 6: Akureyri to West Iceland

    Discover Akureyri, the capital of the north, and witness the majestic Godafoss, famously referred to as the "Waterfall of the Gods." Marvel at the iconic Hvitserkur rock formation and delve into the history of the Settlement Center in Borgarnes. Rest for the night in West Iceland.

    Day 7: West Iceland to Reykjavik

    Experience the awe-inspiring Deildartunguhver, renowned as Europe's most powerful hot spring, and marvel at the extraordinary lava waterfalls of Hraunfossar and Barnafoss. Conclude your remarkable journey with a visit to the esteemed Thingvellir National Park, a distinguished UNESCO World Heritage Site, before returning to the vibrant city of Reykjavik.
